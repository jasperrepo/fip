Touching is healing; from a mothers touch which comforts

health care professionals as being an effective health care alternative.

Massage Therapy is the kneading, manipulation or application of methodical pressure and friction of the body.
Whether it is for treatment of injury, stress or for relaxation,
the skilled use of massage through touch is used to maintain and enhance
the health and well being of the entire body.
